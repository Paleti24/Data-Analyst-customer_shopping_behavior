Project Overview
This end-to-end retail analytics project transforms raw consumer shopping data into actionable business insights.
The goal is to help a retail company:
•	Increase revenue
•	Improve customer engagement
•	Optimize marketing strategies
•	Strengthen long-term customer loyalty
Business Problem
How can consumer shopping data be leveraged to identify purchasing trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?
The company observed changing purchasing patterns across demographics, product categories, and sales channels (online vs. offline). This project analyzes those patterns to identify key drivers of repeat purchases and revenue growth.
Tech Stack
Python – Data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory analysis
SQL – Structured business analysis & customer segmentation
Power BI – Interactive dashboard & KPI visualization
GitHub – Version control & project documentation
Key Analysis Areas
•	Customer segmentation by demographics
•	Revenue analysis by sales channel (Online vs Offline)
•	Repeat purchase behavior
•	Discount impact on purchase decisions
•	Seasonal buying trends
•	Payment method preferences
•	Review ratings vs customer loyalty




Key Insights
Specific demographic segments contribute disproportionately to revenue.
Online and offline channels show distinct purchasing behaviors.
Discounts increase purchase frequency but may reduce profit margins.
Higher review ratings correlate with stronger repeat purchase behavior.
Seasonal trends significantly influence demand patterns.
Business Recommendations
Implement targeted marketing campaigns for high-value customer segments.
Optimize discount strategies using behavioral insights.
Strengthen digital engagement to increase repeat purchases.
Develop data-driven loyalty programs.
Align promotions and inventory planning with seasonal trends.
